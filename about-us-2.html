About the Owner

Nikki Fitzsimmons

I'm Nikki Fitzsimmons, Beauty Boss and owner of Pink Glow Facial Room. I knew I wanted to work in a salon ever since I was a young girl.
After working in a hair studio doing hair and nails for over a decade (I earned my cosmetology degree in 1993), I found my passion for skincare. I went back to school for esthetics in NYC, learning facials and body treatments. I have worked in a high end spa in NJ for 16 years, performing luxury esthetician services, as well as specializing in hair removal. I have since had the opportunity to work for an MD in medical aesthetics, further expanding my already vast skillset when it comes to your skin's specific needs. The pandemic has blessed me with the opportunity to open up Pink Glow!
When I'm not pampering your skin, I am a mom to an amazing teenage boy who is my best friend. I am an avid gardener, love nature, the beach, hiking, and bike riding. Most of all, I love my family and my two little dogs.
